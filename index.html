Rise Beyond

Rise Beyond, tells the story of Ethan Carter (Lyan Taylor), who, as a teenager, decides to leave his hometown and embark on a journey to the big city to fulfill his dream of becoming a famous singer. Along the way, he faces the toughest obstacles that lead him to meet his future band of musicians.
